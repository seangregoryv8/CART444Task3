FOUNDATION_MODULE_04

Layers

Layers are our acetate sheets--each one holds a purpose, and together they build depth without muddying the message.

Layer history

Borrow from analog workflows: paper, tape, overlays, and light tables. The best layering feels like careful collage, not a pile of stickers.

Applied Layering example

Use a scrim behind modals like dimming the room for a projector slide. Elevate the active surface, soften the background, and keep focus locked to the top layer.

Layer tokens

Define a simple z-index scale (base, raised, overlay, modal, toast) and never freestyle it. Tokens make stacking order feel like a labeled rack, not a mystery.
